Sample Vendor/Service Agreement (IT Services – India SME)
VENDOR / SERVICE AGREEMENT
This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into on this 10th day of March 2026 at Hyderabad, Telangana, India.
BETWEEN
XYZ Startups Private Limited
A company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, having CIN: U72900TG2023PTC123456, registered office at Suite 201, Cyber Towers, Madhapur, Hyderabad, Telangana – 500081, represented by its Director, Ms. Priya Mehta (hereinafter referred to as the "Client" or "Company", which expression shall include its successors and permitted assigns) of the ONE PART;
AND
TechSolutions Freelance Services
Proprietorship firm owned by Mr. Vikram Singh, aged 35 years, residing at House No. 12, Jubilee Hills, Hyderabad, Telangana – 500033, holding PAN: DEFPS5678G, GSTIN: 36DEFPS5678G1Z2 (hereinafter referred to as the "Vendor" or "Service Provider", which expression shall include its heirs, executors, administrators and permitted assigns) of the OTHER PART.
WHEREAS
A. The Client is a startup engaged in e-commerce operations and requires custom software development and maintenance services.
B. The Vendor has represented that it has the necessary expertise, resources, and qualifications to provide such IT services.
C. The parties wish to set forth the terms under which the Vendor shall provide services to the Client.
NOW THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants herein, the parties agree as follows:
1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
1.1 The Vendor shall provide the following services ("Services"):
a) Development of a custom e-commerce web application (frontend in React, backend in Node.js).
b) Monthly maintenance, bug fixes, and updates post-launch.
c) Integration with payment gateways (Razorpay) and logistics APIs.
1.2 Deliverables:

Phase 1: UI/UX design & prototype – due within 30 days.
Phase 2: Full development & testing – due within 90 days from start.
Ongoing: Monthly support reports and uptime ≥ 99%.
1.3 The Vendor shall perform Services in a professional manner, adhering to industry standards.

2. TERM
2.1 This Agreement commences on 1st April 2026 and continues for an initial term of 12 (twelve) months.
2.2 Auto-renewal: Automatically renews for successive 6-month periods unless either party gives 60 days' written notice of non-renewal.
2.3 Lock-in: Client cannot terminate without cause in the first 6 months.
3. FEES & PAYMENT
3.1 Total project fee for development: ₹4,50,000 (Rupees Four Lakhs Fifty Thousand only) + 18% GST.
3.2 Maintenance: ₹15,000 per month + GST (post-launch).
3.3 Payment schedule:

30% advance on signing.
40% on prototype approval.
30% on final delivery & acceptance.
Monthly maintenance invoiced on the 1st of each month.
3.4 Late payments attract 1.5% interest per month. All payments via bank transfer / UPI.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
4.1 Both parties shall keep confidential all business, technical, and client data disclosed.
4.2 Obligation survives for 3 (three) years after termination.
5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
5.1 Upon full payment, the Vendor assigns all IP rights (code, designs, documentation) in the deliverables to the Client.
5.2 Vendor grants a perpetual, royalty-free license for any pre-existing tools used.
6. TERMINATION
6.1 Either party may terminate for material breach with 30 days' notice to cure.
6.2 Client may terminate for convenience after initial 6 months with 60 days' notice and payment of outstanding fees.
6.3 Vendor may terminate unilaterally if payments are overdue by 45 days (no notice required after reminder).
6.4 On termination, Vendor shall deliver all work-in-progress and cease Services.
7. INDEMNITY
7.1 Vendor indemnifies Client against third-party claims arising from Vendor's breach, negligence, or infringement of IP.
7.2 Client indemnifies Vendor against claims arising from Client-provided data or instructions.
8. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
8.1 Neither party liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages.
8.2 Total liability capped at fees paid in the preceding 12 months.
9. PENALTY / LIQUIDATED DAMAGES
9.1 If Vendor delays delivery beyond 15 days (without force majeure), penalty of ₹5,000 per week.
9.2 Client pays late fee interest as per Clause 3.4.
10. NON-COMPETE
10.1 For 12 months post-termination, Vendor shall not provide similar services to Client's direct competitors (listed in Schedule A).
11. FORCE MAJEURE
Neither party liable for delays due to events beyond control (e.g., floods, government restrictions, cyber-attacks not caused by negligence).
12. GOVERNING LAW & DISPUTE RESOLUTION
12.1 Governed by laws of India.
12.2 Disputes resolved first by negotiation, then arbitration in Hyderabad under Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 (sole arbitrator).
12.3 Courts in Hyderabad have exclusive jurisdiction.
13. MISCELLANEOUS
13.1 Entire Agreement.
13.2 Amendments in writing only.
13.3 Severability.
13.4 Notices via email/registered post.
